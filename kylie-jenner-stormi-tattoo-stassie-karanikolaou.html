Kylie Jenner Gets New Stormi Tattoo Dedicated to Her Daughter

March 2024 Â· 3 minute read
  • Kylie Jenner's newest tattoo is a touching dedication to her daughter, Stormi Webster.
  • The reality television star shared a video to her Instagram Story to show off her delicate tattoo that says "Stormi."
  • Jenner's best friend, Anastasia "Stassie" Karanikolaou, was also in the video and showed off her matching "Stormi" ink.

Within the past five months, Kylie Jenner's daughter, Stormi Webster, has received an array of opulent presents from the entire Kardashian-Jenner clan.

Aunt Kim Kardashian gifted her a mini Louis Vuitton handbag, and Kylie took her daughter on a tropical vacation with ex-BFF Jordyn Woods. She also threw her a wild Astro World-inspired first birthday party.

However, the first-time mother is giving her daughter a surprise special present that's certainly permanent. Last night, Jenner revealed on her Instagram Story that she got her daughter's name tattooed on her arm.

The tiny namesake tattoo is a few inches above Jenner's elbow, according to her Instagram Story video. One of her fan accounts (@thekyliemood) captured the clip. The footage also reveals that Jenner's best friend Stassie Karanikolaou got the same ink.

"Stop showing it," Karanikolaou teased Kylie in the video, who captioned the video with: "that tattoo scab."

Jenner already has six tiny tattoos, according to Glamour, which would make her latest addition her seventh (that we know of).

Although the beauty mogul has yet to reveal what exactly prompted her to get the "Stormi" body art, it's nonetheless a touching tribute to her only child with rapper-boyfriend Travis Scott.

The family of three spent Mother's Day enjoying a private dinner by the ocean (at what appears to be Nobu in Malibu), according to Jenner's Instagram Story, via @kyliesnapchat. She also received hundreds of pink and white flowers and a pink crocodile Hermes handbag to add to her massive collection.
