They probably agreed on co-parenting their daughter but now Zoe has passed away, Brennan might be getting full custody of their daughter. If you are here that means you are aware that Zoe passed away in a vehicle accident that had her brain damaged which took her life.

Brennan hasn’t said anything about Zoe’s death as of this writing.

Were Zoe Sozo Bethel And Brennan Franklin Married?

Likely, Zoe Sozo Bethel and Brennan Franklin never tied the knots. However, in April 2018, she had referred to Brennan as her “hubby”. There is no evidence of Zoe and Brennan getting engaged or tying the knots while she was alive. So, they broke up before the thought of popping “the big question” even occurred to Brennan.

Brennan Franklin Job

In April 2014, Brennan Franklin landed the job of Kitchen Staff at Chick-fil-A Restaurants. He worked in the restaurant for 8 months.

A year later in April 2015, Brennan joined as Front Service Clerk at Publix Super Markets based in Daphne, Alabama. In this position at Publix, he was responsible for many things all over the store. His main duty was to engage with and help our customers with their needs or questions, and find the best possible solution. He was also responsible for keeping the store looking clean and well maintained from day to day.

In February 2016, Brennan also became Bakery Clerk in the store. He worked in that role for 4 months and was responsible for keeping the sales floor neatly stocked, engaging and helping customers with whatever they may need, and taking orders/requests over the phone.

He is currently working as Front Service Clerk at Publix, according to his LinkedIn.

Brennan attended Mt. Spokane High School followed by Riverpoint Academy. But, he graduated from Spanish Fort High School in 2015. Then, he enrolled at the University of South Alabama to study accounting and business/management. He graduated with a BBA degree in 2019.

He shared on his LinkedIn bio, “I am currently enrolled at the University of South Alabama Mitchell College of Business. By 2021, I hope to have obtained my CPA and start work in auditing. I am from Spokane, WA but reside in Spanish Fort, AL since March of 2014.”

Brennan had also done volunteering work at Timberview Christian Fellowship from September 2012 to February 2014.
