Lance Reddick Biography

Lance Reddick is an American actor and musician. Reddick is well known for playing Phillip Broyles in Fringe and Cedric Daniels in The Wire. Reddick is also known for portraying Detective Johnny Basil on Oz and Matthew Abaddon in Lost. Reddick also provided both the voice and likeness for video game characters Sylens in Horizon Zero Dawn, Martin Hatch in Quantum Break and Commander Zavala in the Destiny franchise.

Lance Reddick Age

Reddick was born on December 31, 1962 in Baltimore, Maryland, U.S. He is 56 years old as of 2018.

Lance Reddick Net Worth

The American actor and musician, Lance Reddick has a net worth of $3 million U.S dollars. His acting career is the primary source of his income. Reddick is known for his portrayal as Phillip Broyles in Fringe and Cedric Daniels in The Wire.

Lance Reddick Education

Reddick went to Friends School of Baltimore. He studied music at the Peabody Preparatory Institute as a teenager and a summer program teaching music theory and composition at the Walden School. After attending the Eastman School of Music at the University of Rochester, Reddick moved to Boston, Massachusetts in the 1980s and enrolled in the Yale School of Drama in 1991.

Lance Reddick The Wire

In the HBO series The Wire, Reddick was cast as Cedric Daniels after having also auditioned for the roles of Bubbles and Bunk Moreland.

Lance Reddick Lost

In 2008, he joined ABC’s hit series Lost, where he played Matthew Abaddon, an employee of Charles Widmore, in multiple episodes. Reddick was the third of five actors from the HBO hit series Oz to star in the drama. Producers Carlton Cuse and Damon Lindelof have said they were interested in Reddick for the part of Mr. Eko but he was unavailable due to filming The Wire, instead they approached Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje.

In 2007, Reddick released his debut album Contemplations & Remembrances and he was cast in early 2008 in a key role for the pilot of Fringe in which fellow Oz actor Kirk Acevedo also had a regular role. He played Phillip Broyles, the head of an FBI department investigating paranormal activities. He described the hard-driving character as “a real hard-ass, but he’s also one of the good guys.” Like Lost, Fringe is co-created and produced by J. J. Abrams.

Reddick was doubt whether he could appear in both Lost and Fringe in the 2008–2009 television season. However, Abrams stated that while being a series regular on Fringe, Reddick would do episodes of Lost whenever required. Reddick appeared in the 2013 thriller White House Down. He is a co-producer in the YouTube web series DR0NE and also stars in it.

In the 2014 video game Destiny, Reddick voiced the character Commander Zavala. Reddick also portrays the role of Chief Irvin Irving in the Amazon Prime series Bosch. He appeared as Charon in the action thriller film John Wick in 2014. Reddick reprises his role in the sequels. Reddick was cast in the post-apocalyptic horror film The Domestics in 2016 which was released on June 28, 2018. In the 2017 video game Horizon: Zero Dawn also Reddick voiced the character Sylens. Reddick was cast in the upcoming film Godzilla vs. Kong In 2018.
