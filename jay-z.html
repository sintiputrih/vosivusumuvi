Jay-Z Wiki/Biography

Born on 04 December 1969, Jay-Z’s age is 52 years as of August 2021. He was born and brought up in a middle-class Christian family from Brooklyn, New York City, the United States. He is an American by nationality and belongs to the Christianity religion.
