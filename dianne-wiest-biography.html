Who is Dianne Wiest?

Dianne Wiest is an award-winning American actress.

She has won the Academy Award twice in her career for Hannah and Her Sisters in 1986 and for Bullets over Broadway in 1994. Some of her notable films include The Purple Rose of Cairo, Radio Days, September, and a few more.

Additionally, she has also starred in the hit series Law & Order, and currently, she is playing Joan Short in Life in Pieces. Moreover, the Oscar Award-winning actress has also received two Primetime Emmy Awards.
