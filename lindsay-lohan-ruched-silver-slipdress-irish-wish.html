Lindsay Lohan Wears Ruched Silver Slipdress for &quot;Irish Wish&quot; Screening

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Lindsay Lohan continued her press-run style streak Tuesday night at a special screening of her upcoming film Irish Wish.

The actor made an entrance at the Paris Theater in New York City in a glamorous vintage gown by John Galliano, sourced from Albright Fashion Library. It was a silky silver slipdress with a deep V neckline and ruched detailing along the center of the bodice, which was broken up by pretty ribbons going from the bust to the waist.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

The sleeveless gown was the centerpiece of Lohan’s red-carpet look, and she paired it with a cozy white double-breasted coat, strappy golden crystal-embellished sandals by Santoni, and layers of gold, silver, and diamond jewelry—dangly chandelier earrings, a watch, tennis bracelets, chunky chain bracelets, rings—disregarding the long-standing fashion rule about not mixing metals.

James Devaney//Getty Images

Lohan also wore one very meaningful piece around her neck: a gold necklace with the name Luai, in honor of her baby son. Lohan and husband Bader Shammas welcomed their first child last year, in Dubai. “The family is over the moon in love,” the actor’s rep confirmed to PopSugar in July. Luai is an Arabic name meaning “shield or protector.”

Gotham//Getty Images

The new parents left their baby at home last night, as Shammas came out to support his wife at the screening. The financier was seen walking behind Lohan as they left the event, looking dapper in a sleek black suit and crisp white dress shirt with no tie.
