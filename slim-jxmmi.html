Slim Jxmmi (born December 29, 1993) is an American Rapper and Musical Artist from Inglewood, California. He is famous for performing alongside his brother Swae Lee as a musical duo named “Rae Sremmurd“. Furthermore, the 25-year-old rapper signed his first record deal with Mike Will Made Its EarDrummers imprint.

As a duo, he and his brother released their debut album titled “SremmLife” in January 2015. Moreover, the album also certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). Slim’s real full birth name is Aaquil Iben Shamon Brown.

Later, In August 2016, Slim Jxmmi and Lee released their 2nd album “SremmLife 2“. In addition, Rae Sremmurd also established their own record label “SremmLife Crew Records” in 2016. Jxmmi’s single “Black Beatles” from Sremmlife 2 gained number 1 position on the US Billboard Hot 100.

On January 11, 2019, Slim performed with Nicki Minaj in Australia along with Swae. However, he later tweeted on his official account that he is not a member of Rae Sremmurd anymore. Later, he deleted all the posts from his page.
