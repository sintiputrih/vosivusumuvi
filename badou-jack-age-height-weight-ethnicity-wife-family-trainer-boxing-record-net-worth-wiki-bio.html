Professional boxer Badou Johannes Gabriel Jack is Swedish-Gambian and was born on October 31, 1983.

He held the WBC super-middleweight title from 2014 to 2017 and the WBA light-heavyweight belt in 2017.

He was a previous world champion in two weight classes. He competed in the middleweight division as an amateur for The Gambia at the 2008 Olympics, making it to the opening round.

Badou Jack Wikipedia & Biography

At the age of 18, Jack started his amateur boxing career in 2001.

His record was 150 victories and 25 defeats. From 2004 to 2008, he won Sweden’s national title five times in a row, four times at middleweight and one time at light heavyweight.

The Swedish Boxing Federation awarded Jack the boxer of the year in 2007.

In the finals of his last national championship, which he won in 2008, he overcame Erik Skoglund, a fellow rising star.

He competed for Sweden at light-heavyweight before switching to Gambia and earning a spot in the 2008 Olympics.
