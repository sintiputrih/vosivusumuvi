Melissa Courchain dies after hard battle with cystic fibrosis: obituary

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Hainesport, NJ – On Monday, December 4, 2023, the sudden passing of Melissa Courchain left countless hearts heavy with grief. Her unexpected departure has deeply affected all those fortunate enough to have crossed paths with her. Tributes and condolences have poured in for Melissa Courchain’s family, highlighting the impact she made on many lives. Amid this profound loss, everyone is encouraged to support her family during this challenging time.

Susan Dawn posted on Facebook,

“I’m absolutely heartbroken to learn my sweet little sparkplug of a friend, that I met when I was 11, has lost her life long battle with cystic fibrosis. The odds were stacked against her for years… decades.. and she fought so hard and won for soooo long. “Melissa Courchain lit up a room with her dancing, wiggles and laugh. Despite her condition, she was an amazing ATHLETE! A singer and dancer. I played field hockey and softball with her from middle school days through high school. She was an amazing wife to her husband Bob and a momma to little Austin. I knew her family well back in the day. She also had an epic group of girlfriends that have all been friends since they were children, and still are to this day.
“I’m sad to be so far away during this hard time. I wish I could hug and cry with everyone.
Please stay tuned to how you can help the family in the coming days and weeks. Support platforms are in the works as I type this. Please check her page, (as well as mine and Tracy Fleming and others to be named ) we are taking special care to create the proper ways to support her family.
“Giggle and wiggle in heaven Guig. We all love you so much. You left a mark on everyone’s heart you ever said hello to.”

Melissa Courchain was a luminous presence, infusing joy and vitality into every space she entered. Her passion for dance, infectious laughter, and unwavering positivity defined her character. A talented athlete, she exuded resilience regardless of the circumstances. Known for her prowess in dance and vocals, Melissa was cherished by her husband, Bob, and their son, Austin. She shared enduring friendships, spanning from childhood to the present day.

Her battle against cystic fibrosis was one of incredible courage and perseverance. Susan Dawn paid tribute, acknowledging Melissa’s unwavering fight against overwhelming odds for years. Her resilience was a testament to her strength and determination, inspiring those who knew her.

Melissa Courchain’s family will release details about her obituary and funeral arrangements as they navigate through this difficult time. Her memory will forever remain in the hearts of those who were fortunate to know her.
