  • Gigi Hadid and The Bachelorette's Tyler Cameron appear to be spending a lot of time together; the pair apparently just went on a trip to upstate New York.

Gigi Hadid's new romance appears to be going very well.

Related Story

The Victoria's Secret model has been linked to The Bachelorette's Tyler Cameron, and it seems like they've already taken their first mini getaway together.

Page Six reported that Cameron and Hadid were spotted in a Starbucks near Lake George in upstate New York on August 15. A source also reportedly told the publication that Hadid enjoyed another Starbucks that same day in Malta, New York, but the model was with "two or three girls" on that occasion.

Judging by Instagram user @emily_visco13's snap, Hadid and Cameron were super chill during their Starbucks date.

Earlier this week, a source reportedly told E! News, "There is a connection there for sure." The source continued, "Gigi was smiling more last night than she has in a while. She just looks really happy and calm with Tyler around. It's like she is herself around him, no pretense or act."

However, another source revealed, "They are both very into each other but Gigi has been really hesitant about getting serious with him." But could that be about to change during their road trip together?
