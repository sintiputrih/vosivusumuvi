Emily Ratajkowski Went From Pantless to Shirtless in Paris

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski is conquering Paris Fashion Week one risqué trend at a time. After kicking things off at the Courrèges show in an ab-baring latex crop top and low-rise midi skirt, the model and author took the pantless trend for a spin before doing a full sartorial 180 and ditching her shirt less than a day later.  

First, Ratajkowski was spotted taking a break from the shows while strolling the streets in a sculpted black long-sleeved sweater with oversized sleeves and a micro-mini hemline. She left her legs bare and added a pair of Mary-Jane flats, a tiny handbag, and oval-shaped sunglasses to her look, while a black leather jacket draped effortlessly over her arm. Big curls and a nude makeup palette rounded out her glam. 

The next morning, EmRata attended the Loewe fall-winter 2024 runway show, and rather than no pants this time around, she switched things up and went shirtless underneath her unbuttoned navy blazer that showed off her washboard abs. She paired the jacket with knee-length black leather shorts with a gold sewing needle sliced through the waistband and accessorized with strappy black heels and double cat-eye sunglasses with tortoiseshell and gold frames. 

Surprisingly (or maybe unsurprisingly), these weren't the only sexy outfits Ratajkowski wore within a 24-hour period. Sneaking a third look into the mix, the podcast host shared a carousel of images her wearing a naked dress in an Instagram post casually captioned: "last night." In the photos, she wore nothing but a black thong underneath a see-through gold netted gown that featured a fringed hemline and a matching skinny scarf wrapped around her neck.
