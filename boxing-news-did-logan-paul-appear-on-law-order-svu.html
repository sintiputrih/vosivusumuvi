Did Logan Paul Appear on Law &amp; Order SVU?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Logan Paul, the internet’s favorite punching bag, has appeared in several shows and movies over the years. Every time influencers make a name for themselves and grow in fame and influence, it opens up opportunities to work in the Film and television industry. Many influencers have opted to venture onto this path.

Logan Paul is no different. ‘The Maverick’ has a few movie appearances and shows to his name. However, one of his most surprising cameos was in season 16 of the popular TV drama, Law & Order SVU.

Paul made a cameo in the series that stunned Law & Order and Logan Paul fans alike.

Is Logan Paul on law and order?

Logan Paul played a cameo in season 16 of Law & Order in an episode named, “Intimidation Game.” The storyline of the episode is the hunt of Gamers that threaten a popular video game developer just before the launch of her game.

Paul plays the villain, the head of the gamer guys that threaten the developer.

Logan Paul appeared in Season 16 of SVU

In his cameo appearance, Paul gets shot by Ice-T in the end. Ice-T is one of the most popular characters on the show and Paul was oblivious to this when he came on set. Law & Order is the longest-running TV drama yet. For Paul to play a cameo in the series was a great honor.

In an interview with Aol. Paul opened up about his experience on set.

Logan opens up about his experience working with Ice-T and the rest of SVU’s squad

The interviewer for Aol. asked Paul what it felt like to work on the set of Law & Order. “It was awesome! I was here in New York and it was great. I got, spoiler alert, shot by Ice-T, I actually have a funny story about that. So Ice-T is apparently a big deal and I am not part of the Ice-T generation.”

“So he comes on set wearing a cop outfit and goes, “Yo, what’s up are you the bad guy?” And I say, “Yeah! Are you the security guard on set today?”
