Demi Moore &amp; Bruce Willis' Daughter Tallulah Announces Her Engagement to Dillon Buss

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Demi Moore and Bruce Willis’s daughter Tallulah announced her engagement to her boyfriend, Dillon Buss. The youngest daughter flaunted her blinding diamond ring on social media.

Tallulah Willis is now a soon-to-be bride! Yesterday, the daughter of Demi Moore and Bruce Willis announced her engagement to longtime boyfriend Dillon Buss via social media with an array of heartwarming photos.

The first upload shared showed Buss getting down on one knee in front of what looked like her home. Tallulah, dressed in a simple pink gown, jumped excitedly on her man, after which they exchanged kisses.

Tallulah Willis attends The Art Of Elysium's 11th Annual Celebration on January 6, 2018 in Santa Monica, California. | Photo: Getty Images

She captioned the photo, “With absolute most certainty.” In a separate post, the 27-year-old gave fans a close glimpse at her gigantic engagement ring as her hands shook nervously. She wrote:

“HANDS STILL SHAKIN’ — MOMS SPAGHETTI — I’m FIANCÉNCHED.”

Buss also shared a photo of him and Tallulah cuddling, confessing in the caption that he was glad to be able to call her his fiancée finally. According to reports, the ring was custom-created by Brooklyn jeweler Karina Noel.

The blinding piece is an emerald-cut center stone ring with beveled edges and an accompanying yellow gold band. Tallulah’s sisters, Scout and Rumer, celebrated the good news with posts on social media.

While it’s not sure how long the lovebirds have been dating, “The Whole Nine Yards” star never shies away from celebrating their unique love on social media. Back in August, the youngest of the Willis girls lauded Buss for coming through for her.

In a social media post, the actress explained that she was having a mental breakdown and needed some time alone. However, Buss surprised her with a picnic basket filled with some of her favorite snacks.
