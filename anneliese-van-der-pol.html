Anneliese van der Pol is a Dutch actress who is a famous Broadway performer and is best known for appearing as Chelsea on the show, That’s So Raven. She is a critically acclaimed artist who has performed as Belle in Beauty and the Beast and as Kathy in Vanities, A New Musical. She has a huge social media fan base with more than 500k followers on Instagram, more than 100k followers on Twitter, and more than 300k followers on Facebook.

Anneliese went to Washington Elementary School in Bellflower, California, after her family shifted base from the Netherlands to America.

    Anneliese van der Pol Facts

  • She is named after the German-born diarist Anne Frank after her mother visited the house where Anne Frank hid from the Nazis.
  • She holds dual American and Dutch citizenship.
  • Anneliese started acting while she was in the 3rd grade.
  • Anneliese earned herself a Bobbi Award under the “Best Actress in a Musical” category after she played the role of Sandy Dumbrowsky in the Huntington Beach Playhouse revival of Grease in 1999.
  • After she played Eva Perón in the Buena Park Civic Theatre production of the musical Evita in 1999, Anneliese gained positive reviews from the Los Angeles Times and also became the youngest actress in the history of theatrical performances to play the role in a professional production.
  • She earned an Austin Critics Table Award nomination under the “Best Actress in a Musical” category and a B. Iden Payne nomination under the “Best Featured Actress in a Musical” category after performing “Laurey” in an Austin, Texas musical theatre production of Oklahoma! from 2000 to 2001.
  • She turned down the lead role of Kim Possible in the animated series of the same name in 2002 which was then performed by Christy Carlson Romano.
  • Anneliese was the only cast member of That’s So Raven who was not an American.
  • She has collaborated with Disney for their various music projects including the theme song of That’s So Raven in 2003.
  • Anneliese made her Broadway debut on April 3, 2007, as Belle in Beauty and the Beast.
  • On July 10, 2006, Anneliese got arrested in Los Angeles for DUI as she had a blood-alcohol level more than the legal limit in California. She was sentenced for the crime in 2007 and was ordered to pay fines, go on probation, and attend an alcohol education program.
  • Anneliese has been featured on the covers of magazines like Disney Adventures (US) and Playbill (US) in June 2003 and July 2009 respectively.
