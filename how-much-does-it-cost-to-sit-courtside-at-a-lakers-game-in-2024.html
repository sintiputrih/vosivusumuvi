How much does it cost to sit courtside at a Lakers game in 2024?

Sitting courtside at a Los Angeles Lakers game is a dream for many basketball fans and celebrities alike. The allure of being up close to the action and rubbing shoulders with A-listers comes at a hefty price. In 2024, the cost of courtside Lakers tickets varies considerably based on several factors, including the game's demand, the opponent's quality, the day of the week, home vs. away venues, and whether it's the regular season or the playoffs.

Factors Affecting Courtside Ticket Prices

The cost of courtside tickets for Lakers games is influenced by various factors:

  • Game Demand: High-profile matchups against formidable opponents like the Golden State Warriors or Memphis Grizzlies tend to command higher prices, while games against lower-ranking teams may have more affordable courtside options.

  • Day of the Week: Games scheduled for Tuesday or Wednesday nights generally offer cheaper courtside tickets compared to weekend games.

  • Home vs. Away Games: Consider checking out away games if you are looking to see the Lakers courtside for a more pocket-friendly price. For example, courtside tickets for the Los Angeles Lakers at Oklahoma City Thunder can be found for as low as $700.00.

  • Regular Season vs. Playoffs: The cost of courtside tickets may also vary during the regular season and playoffs.

    • Price Range for Courtside Tickets

    The price range for courtside Lakers tickets in 2024 is as follows:

    • Cheapest at-home courtside tickets: Starting around $5,500.00 for games against lower-ranking teams or those on Tuesday or Wednesday nights.
    • Higher-profile matchups: Tickets for games against stiff competition like the Golden State Warriors or Memphis Grizzlies can range from about $9,000.00 to $42,000.00 per ticket.
    • Regular season average: The average ticket price per game this season is around $451.27, with prices as low as $17.60 and as high as $33,272.00.

    Historical Ticket Prices

    Los Angeles Lakers courtside tickets have been known to command significant prices. In the past, these coveted seats have seen season passes costing well over $200,000, and individual courtside tickets easily costing $3,000 or more.

    Availability of Courtside Seats

    The Lakers have a relatively small amount of courtside seating, with just two rows of courtside seats that wrap around the court. Due to the limited availability of these seats, they tend to go for a substantial amount of money.

    Where to Purchase Courtside Tickets

    If you're interested in purchasing courtside tickets for the Los Angeles Lakers, reputable ticket platforms like Vivid Seats and SeatGeek offer a range of options for fans looking to secure these coveted seats.

    Tips for Finding Affordable Courtside Tickets

    For those looking for more affordable courtside options, the following tips might be helpful:

    • Preseason and Mid-Week Games: Preseason games and certain mid-week games might offer the best options for cheap courtside tickets.
    • Away Games: Considering attending away games for potentially more affordable courtside ticket prices.
