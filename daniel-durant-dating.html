Where Is Britt Stewart from?

Britt Stewart was born to her father and mother on September 21, 1989, in her hometown, Aurora, Colorado.

Stewart stands tall, with a height of 5 feet 7 inches. Similarly, Britt's last recorded weight was around 55 kg.
