January Jones Wears Jumpsuit With Huge Keyhole Cutout: Pics

March 2024 · 2 minute read

How low can she go? January Jones rocked the most daring of keyhole cutouts at the annual CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund Show and Tea on Wednesday, October 26, in L.A.

The Mad Men alum, 38, took the plunge in a coral Cushnie Et Ochs halter jumpsuit with a cutout at the bodice so deep, it flirted with the potential for a wardrobe malfunction. Fortunately, no such thing happened to Jones, who teamed the style with a contrasting two-toned envelope clutch by Sophie Hulme, Steven Alan Optical sunglasses and taupe ankle-strap sandals.

Adding even more sass to her ensemble, the Last Man on Earth actress modeled feathered curls and an eye-popping hot-pink statement lip.

Jones was joined at the event by Emma Stone, Katy Perry, Nicole Richie, Alessandra Ambrosio and Ciara, making her first appearance since announcing her pregnancy on Tuesday, October 25.

While Jones looked flawless, she revealed via Instagram that the road to beauty was paved with at least one wild — and inspiring! — hair moment. “Took my hair out of the clip it had been in all day and this happened,” she captioned. “What an unexpected surprise, I was delighted.” Why? Because, as she noted, the style served up “#tildavibes.” But she also added, “#timeforsomeshampoo.”
