What’s the Story in Season 2?

In a world where magic and science coexist, Season 2 follows Adonis, raised by the Ice Witch Chloe. After witnessing his foster mother’s execution, Adonis is fueled by grief and a strong desire for revenge against the Redians. The story takes a perilous turn as Adonis and the last surviving witch, Doroka, navigate dangerous landscapes, encountering diverse characters each with their motivations and challenges.
