How Many Siblings Does Shauna Rae Have? I Am Shauna Rae Star Talks Sisters and Family

May 2024 · 3 minute read

TV Star Shauna Rae has three siblings, one elder sister Tara and two half-siblings Rylee and Morgan. Find more about the actress.

‘I Am Shauna Rae’ portrays the lifestyle of 22 years old Shauna Rae and her daily activities.

Shauna was treated for an extremely rare form of brain cancer when she was just six months old.

The side effect of this remediation was that her pituitary gland was affected and the release of growth hormones was halted.

This caused the now 22 years old girl to get trapped in the body of an 8 years old, medically coined term ‘pituitary dwarf’.

The current show called “I Am Shauna Rae” showcases her personal thoughts, daily struggles, robust personality traits, and joyous nature to the general audience.

However, there has been a stronghold of both critical fans and supportive fans for the young lady.

The University of Pennsylvania graduate is a local resident in the Pittsburg region and features as the lead cast of the TLC airing documentary program.

How Many Siblings Does Shauna Rae Have?

TV documentary star Shauna Rae has three siblings from her parent’s double multiple marriages.

Shauna was born in 1999 to her father Scott Resnick and her mother Patricia Schrankel.

Shauna is also looked after by her stepfather Mark Schrankel in the show.

She was raised alongside her sister Tara Resnick, born to their real father Scott Resnick.

Similarly, the 22 years old star has two other half-sisters or half-siblings from their other mother’s side.

Shauna and Tara grew up in a Pittsburg-friendly neighborhood.

Tara relocated to the Tallahassee region of Florida and pursued her graduate course at Florida State University.

She has been engaged to her love interest Jesse Barricelli.

The family of two parents and three sisters are seen hanging out at times and making some great family trips to remote places and enjoying good vibes.

Meet Shauna Rae’s Sisters Rylee and Morgan From The Reality Show

Morgan Rae and Rylee Rae are Shauna’s half-sisters from the other maternal side.

Rylee is a Longwood High School graduate and lives in their family home while Tara has already moved away for higher education.

Morgan Rae also graduated from the University of Pennsylvania just like her half-sister Shauna.

Morgan works as a consultant at Belmont Charter Network and also serves as an advocate plus creator.

The show perfectly portrays the sibling’s chaotic and pure bond as they spend some time together while driving.

Who Are Shauna Rae’s Parents?
