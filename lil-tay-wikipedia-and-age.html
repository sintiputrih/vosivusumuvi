You might recall the viral young girl, Lil Tay who frequently used to flaunt her lifestyle on social media, along with the shocking news of her passing. Read this article to find out more about her life, career and age.

Lil Tay Wikipedia

Lil Tay  does not have a Wikipedia page yet. However, Lil Tay was a 14-year-old American rapper and internet personality. She was born to her parents in the year 2009 in the United States of America. In 2019, Lil Tay’s presence on social media dwindled due to a custody dispute between her mother, Angela Tian, and her father, Chris Hope.

Angela Tian, who worked as a real estate agent, apparently left her job after it was revealed that she had used vacant homes for Lil Tay’s videos. The involvement of her brother, Jason Tian, in crafting her persona and achieving her fame was also mentioned in reports from The Cut.

On August 9, 2023, Lil Tay’s family announced that she had passed away at the age of 14. A statement was shared on Lil Tay’s Instagram, expressing the profound sorrow and unexpected nature of her passing.

Her family also mentioned the loss of her brother, which added an additional layer of grief. The circumstances around their passing are currently being investigated. The family asked for privacy during this difficult time.

Lil Tay gained fame for her opulent lifestyle showcased in videos, where she used explicit language and engaged in conflicts with other online personalities. According to reports, she claimed to have come from a humble background in Atlanta and mentioned working hard to achieve success.

She had garnered over 3.3 million followers on Instagram and collaborated with figures like Jake Paul and producer Rick Rubin. In a 2018 interview on Good Morning America, she addressed her viral status, mentioning her accomplishments at a young age and her carefree attitude towards skeptics.
