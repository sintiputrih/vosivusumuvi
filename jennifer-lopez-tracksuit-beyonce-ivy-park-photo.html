Jennifer Lopez Models Beyonc's Ivy Park x Adidas Activewear

April 2024 · 2 minute read

If there's one collaboration we'll always be ready for, it's Jennifer Lopez and Beyoncé.

Almost as if the superstars knew that we all needed a little more joy in our lives in 2020, J.Lo decided to showcase some pieces from Beyoncé's latest Ivy Park collection. As expected, Beyoncé's designs look perfect on J.Lo, and it's basically proof that the pair should start cooking up even more creations together in the new year.

Lounging on her couch, Lopez captioned the snaps, "Swaggy Saturday," and credited her fiancé, Alex Rodriguez, as the photographer. Wearing a head to toe glossy gold tracksuit, with Timberland boots, J.Lo showed, once again, that activewear can be chic.

During an interview with Yahoo Life this week, Lopez revealed that maintaining her health and fitness regime has been much more difficult during the pandemic. "I’ve never struggled more with my health and nutrition, and I'm a pretty motivated person," she explained. "But throughout quarantine, I was just, you know, I just felt like some days I was motivated to eat well and work out. Others, I just didn't want to, I just didn't see what the point was."

J.Lo also revealed, "The one thing that I tried to keep constant in our home was to dance and to play outside. Just turning on music and having a dance party. ... Sometimes for, like, 15 minutes with the kids is all you need. Having them step outside from being in front of the computer all day or being on their electronics all day is all you need to do."
