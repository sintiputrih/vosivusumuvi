Charley Hull, a prominent figure in the world of golf, has had a successful career marked by her remarkable achievements in various tournaments. 

LPGA Tour Victories

Charley Hull has clinched two victories on the LPGA Tour:

  • 2022: The Ascendant LPGA benefiting Volunteers of America

    • In 2022, Charley Hull emerged victorious at The Ascendant LPGA benefiting Volunteers of America. 

  • 2016: CME Group Tour Championship

    • Hull's first LPGA Tour victory came at the 2016 CME Group Tour Championship, signifying a significant milestone in her career.

    • Other Notable Wins

    In addition to her LPGA Tour victories, Charley Hull has secured victories in other prestigious tournaments:

    Solheim Cup

    Charley Hull has an impressive track record in the Solheim Cup, having secured victories in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2021, and 2023, showcasing her consistency and prowess in this esteemed event.

    International Crown

    Hull's success extends to the International Crown, where she triumphed in 2016 and 2018, further solidifying her standing as a force to be reckoned with in the world of golf.

    Olympics

    Charley Hull participated in the 2016 Rio Olympics, where she achieved a commendable T7 finish, demonstrating her ability to compete at the highest level of international competition.

    Career Highlights

    2023

    In 2023, Hull participated in 18 events, securing 14 cuts made and earning $2,395,650. She recorded five top-10 finishes, four of which were runner-up results, underscoring her consistent performance on the tour.

    2022

    During the 2022 season, Hull's stellar performance included 18 events, 13 cuts made, and earnings amounting to $1,084,968. She recorded seven top-10 finishes, culminating in a remarkable victory at The Ascendant LPGA benefiting Volunteers of America.

    2021

    In 2021, Charley Hull partook in 19 events, securing 17 cuts made and earning $392,308. She achieved two top-10 finishes, with a season-best fifth place result at the Trust Golf Women's Scottish Open. She also represented the European Solheim Cup Team, further showcasing her prowess in team competitions.

    2020

    During the 2020 season, Hull participated in eight events, securing six cuts made and earning $281,594. She achieved two top-10 finishes, including a season-best T6 result at the Volunteers of America Classic, highlighting her ability to consistently perform at a high level.

    2019

    In 2019, Hull's notable achievements included 22 events, 17 cuts made, and earnings of $885,961. She secured a runner-up position at the season-finale CME Group Tour Championship, demonstrating her competitiveness at elite tournaments. 

    2018

    Charley Hull's performance in 2018 was characterized by participation in 24 events, securing 22 cuts made, and earnings amounting to $869,012. She achieved top-10 finishes at three of the five majors, highlighting her ability to excel on the grandest stages of the sport. Notably, she secured a runner-up position at the LPGA KEB Hana Bank Championship.

    2017

    In 2017, Hull participated in 22 events, securing 18 cuts made and earning $442,942. She recorded three top-10 finishes, including a season-best T6 at the LPGA KEB Hana Bank Championship, showcasing her ability to consistently contend at various events.

     

    Charley Hull's career is adorned with numerous victories and noteworthy performances, both as an individual competitor and as a valuable team member in prestigious events such as the Solheim Cup.

    Her consistent track record and ability to deliver standout performances underscore her status as a prominent figure in the world of professional golf, leaving an indelible mark on the sport through her remarkable tournament victories and career highlights.
