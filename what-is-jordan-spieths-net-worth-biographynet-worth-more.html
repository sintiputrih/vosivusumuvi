Who is Jordan Spieth?

Jordan Alexander Spieth was born July 27, 1993 in Dallas, Texas, United States. His parents are Shawn Spieth and Mary Christine Julius. He attended St. Monica Catholic School before later graduating from Jesuit College Preparatory School in 2011. Spieth was taught to play golf at Brookhaven Country Club.

Spieth participated in the 2015 Masters Tournament and that was where he landed his first major win. He ended the competition with a 270 (-18), tying up the 72-hole record set by former world number one Tiger Woods in 1997 to become the second youngest golfer to win the Masters.

Same year in 2015, Spieth won the U.S. Open with a score of 5-under par. That was another feat he achieved as the youngest U.S. Open Champion since amateur Bobby Jones in 1923. Subsequently, he won the 2015 Tour Championship, which clinched the 2015 FedEx Cup. In 2017, he won his third major at the 2017 Open Championship, by three shots at 12 under par.
