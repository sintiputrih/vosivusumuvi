Tara Reid Threatens Lisa D'Amato on 'Marriage Boot Camp'

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Not seeing eye to eye! Tara Reid confronts Lisa D’Amato and challenges her to a stare down in Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars‘ Friday, July 1, episode, as seen in Us Weekly’s exclusive sneak peek.

The We TV unscripted series’ preview clip shows D’Amato, 35, mentioning to Reid, 40, that she senses tension between the two of them.

The America’s Next Top Model alum tells the camera, “As far as Tara’s concerned, I realize that she’s trying to shut me down from asking her questions, and it was like ice. It was like she was trying to shoot ice through my veins.”

For her part, Reid says to the camera, “Lisa is always jumping into everyone else’s business and criticizing them, but yet she doesn’t [remain open to criticism] herself.”

After D’Amato laments that the American Pie actress doesn’t even give her eye contact, Reid leans toward her and stares directly at her.

“Look in my eyes, Lisa,” the Taradise star says. “Here’s eye contact. What part of this don’t you get? I’m not afraid of you. You want to start a fight? I’m not scared of you. You want to go there? I’m not going to do it.”

When D’Amato says she doesn’t want to fight because she is Reid’s fan, the actress fires back, “Don’t be my fan — be my friend. Don’t even be my friend — be in the house.” Ouch.
